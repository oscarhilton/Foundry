What is Foundry?

Magnetic cubes that snap into a chain — a physical language for behaviours, not a smart-home dashboard.

What do I need?

A Starter Kit: Core, Place, Weather, and Light. Build Place → Weather → Light, then add vocabulary cubes as you go.

Can I configure it?

Yes. Choose your place when setting up your Core — change it later without replacing the cube. Wi-Fi lives in the Core.

A physical language

Nouns. Verbs. Outputs.

Chains read left to right like a sentence. Place scopes context; sources and transforms shape facts; outputs make the sentence visible.

Nouns

Place, Foundry

Context — where or what the sentence is about.

Verbs

Weather, Time, GitHub

Sources and transforms that fetch or reshape facts.

Outputs

Light, Display

Where the sentence becomes visible on your desk.

Foundry is not a smart-home platform.

It is a physical language for behaviours.

  • Sentences execute on the Core
  • Place cubes are programmable — not one cube per city
  • Wi-Fi lives in the Core, not in your sentence
